# 1.2.2 Using CX Enterprise Coworker for insights

Review and reuse the below prompts to get CX Enterprise Coworker's input.

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
If a major competitor launches aggressive pricing next month, which customer segments are most at risk and how should CitiSignal respond?
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
Identify customer segments with the highest untapped revenue potential and recommend monetization strategies.
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
Create a retention strategy for customers showing early signs of disengagement.
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
Analyze our current marketing performance, customer acquisition, content engagement, audience segments, campaign effectiveness, and conversion funnel. Identify the 5 biggest growth opportunities, estimate revenue impact for each, highlight risks, and recommend the top 3 actions I should fund or prioritize in the next 90 days.
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
I am planning a fiber rollout in Europe. Tell me: 1. How many customers in that region currently have usage > 2000 GB/month 2. How many are on legacy broadband plans 3. What is their average spend per month 4. What genres do they watch most (to personalize the campaign) 5. Are any of them already in a fiber promotion journey Give me a go/no-go recommendation for the rollout.
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
We had a network outage on last month in Sweden. Find all customers who were active during that window. Show me: How many were affected, Their average monthly spend (revenue at risk), Whether any have contacted support since, Suggest a proactive retention offer for the most valuable ones
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
Find customers who have only one product (e.g. only Fiber, or only Mobile). Group by: Single product type they currently have, Their usage patterns, Favourite content genre For each group suggest the most logical bundle add-on. Estimate revenue if 10% convert to a bundle.
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
Give me a complete performance review and optimization plan for the "Explore the Super-speed" journey for the last 2 months
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
Help me increase my purchases of X by 10%. Focus on channels email and push.
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
Analyze the data to identify the key dimensions driving the drop in sales between the first and second week of May
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
Analyze the sales performance decline observed between Week 1 (May 1–7) and Week 2 (May 8–14) and identify the primary dimensions, segments, and behavioral drivers contributing to the decrease. Conduct a comprehensive decomposition of the variance by examining: Traffic acquisition dimensions (Marketing Channel, Campaign, Source/Medium, Referrer, Paid vs. Organic), Customer dimensions (New vs. Returning Visitors, Loyalty Status, Audience Segments, Geographic Region, Device Type, Browser, Operating System), Commerce dimensions (Product Category, SKU, Brand, Product Affinity, Cart Activity, Checkout Funnel Stage), Journey dimensions (Entry Page, Landing Page, Exit Page, Internal Search Behavior, Content Engagement, Journey Stage). Experience dimensions (Page Performance, Site Errors, Form Failures, Mobile Experience, App vs. Web Experience). For each significant contributor: Quantify its contribution to the overall sales decline. Measure changes in traffic volume, conversion rate, average order value, revenue per visitor, and purchase frequency. Identify statistically significant anomalies, outliers, or emerging patterns. Compare performance against the prior four-week baseline to determine whether the decline is seasonal, campaign-driven, operational, or behavioral. Highlight interactions between dimensions (e.g., specific channels underperforming within particular regions or devices). Present findings in a ranked executive summary showing: Top positive and negative contributors to revenue. Root-cause analysis of the sales decline. Estimated revenue impact attributable to each driver.
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
Act as a growth and lifecycle marketing analyst for CitiSignal. Compare fiber purchase trends, the target audience definition, and the current Fiber Max journey performance. Identify the biggest gap between business potential and actual results by highlighting: 1) which customer segments are most likely to buy fiber but are not in the current audience, 2) where the journey has the highest fallout, 3) whether the current audience is too narrow or too broad, and 4) what operational conflicts or experiment results may be blocking growth. Conclude with the top 3 actions to close the gap.
```

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
Act as a chief growth strategist for CitiSignal. Using customer purchase behavior, content preferences, existing journeys, and audience performance, identify the highest-lift growth opportunity for Fiber Max. Recommend the single best audience to target, the best offer or message angle, the best journey structure, and the best follow-up strategy to maximize fiber adoption over the next 30 days. Prioritize segments with high bandwidth need, strong upgrade propensity, and low current conversion, and explain where the highest ROI will come from.
```

Which customer segments represent the largest untapped revenue opportunity?

Analyze the current flow, identify engagement gaps, and recommend high-impact assets, personalization strategies, and journey enhancements that improve customer engagement and drive better conversion outcomes.

Enter the following prompt and click **Send**.

```
Review the current CitiSignal Fiber Upgrade Journey, which targets customers eligible for a fiber upgrade and sends a follow-up email. Analyze previous Journey performance and identify opportunities to reduce customer drop-off and increase conversions. Recommend specific changes to audience targeting, messaging, timing, and touchpoints, then update the Journey accordingly. The optimized Journey should aim to: Achieve a Fiber Upgrade conversion rate above 15%, Increase Fiber Upgrade revenue by at least 5% compared to current results, Provide the expected conversion and revenue impact of each recommended change.
