# 1.8 Unified Review & Approval with Workfront, Frame.io and Adobe Cloud Storage

The Workfront + Frame.io integration transforms creative operations from a series of disconnected steps into a single, connected experience. It enables organizations to produce more content, with higher quality, in less time while giving every stakeholder the visibility and confidence they need.

By connecting Workfront’s project and work management capabilities directly with Frame.io’s real-time review and collaboration experience, teams eliminate handoffs, duplicate updates, and status-chasing.

Creative work flows seamlessly from planning into execution and review, allowing teams to:

- Move from brief to final asset with fewer delays
- Reduce review cycles by keeping feedback contextual and centralized
- Keep projects moving without switching tools or losing momentum
