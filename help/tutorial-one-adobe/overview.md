## What is the Content Supply Chain?

The One Adobe tutorial is organized around the 5 phases of the content supply chain which is designed to streamline all steps of your marketing activities from planning a campaign all the way to measuring the performance of your marketing activities. Each Tech Lab is mapped to one of the phases of the content supply chain.

- **Workflow & Planning**: Strategizing and orchestrating content initiatives using tools like Adobe Workfront to align teams, define content, and manage project timelines.

- **Creation & Production**: Producing content assets through applications like Adobe Creative Cloud and generative AI (Adobe Firefly), which automate tasks and speed up production.

- **Asset Management**: Organizing, storing, and making assets accessible via Adobe Experience Manager Assets to avoid duplicative work.

- **Delivery & Activation**: Distributing content across channels (websites, social media) using Dynamic Media for optimized experiences.

- **Reporting & Insights**: Analyzing content performance using Adobe Analytics to gain insights for future strategy.
