# Schedule automated training and scoring for a service

Adobe Experience Platform Data Science Workspace allows you to set up scheduled scoring and training runs on a machine learning service. Automating the training and scoring process can help maintain and improve a service's efficiency through time by keeping up with the different patterns in your data. For more information, please visit the [Data Science Workspace documentation](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/experience-platform/data-science-workspace/home.html).
